SIX SEXES!?

Most people strongly believe that there are only 2 sexes; the presence or absence of the ‘y’ chromosome is what really matters, without it, you are female, and with it, you are male. However, doctors argue that this idea is overly simplistic and some individuals bestride the boundary- their sex chromosomes convey a message different to that showcased by their sexual anatomy or gonads (testes/ovaries), often leading to conditions known as intersex conditions, or differences or disorders of sex development (DSDs). Today, due to advancements in the medical filed, mostly genetics and DNA, we are able to examine the karyotype- an individual’s collection of chromosome- more carefully. The Nature Feature gathers research that has changed the way biologists perceive sex. New technologies in cell biology and DNA sequencing demonstrate that chromosomal sex is a process, not an assignation. Humans are not just born male or female, the presence of at least 6 sexes have been identified that result in a fairly normal life span. It is also believed that there are more than just 6 sexes, but these result in spontaneous abortion, as the body knows that the foetus is not going to be viable.

The six karyotypes that can survive are

• XX – The Most common form of female

• XY – The Most common form of male

• X – Roughly 1 in 2,000 to 1 in 5,000 people (Turner’s )

• XXY – Roughly 1 in 500 to 1 in 1,000 people (Klinefelter)

• XYY – Roughly 1 out of 1,000 people

• XXXY – Roughly 1 in 18,000 to 1 in 50,000 births

Nowadays, the difference between the meaning of biological sex and gender has been misunderstood. Biological sex is determined by the karyotype the individual possesses. The physiology and the brain, reproductive system and body, resembles that of the opposite sex , like in the case of a transgender. Whereas gender is mostly associated with cultural behaviour: the way a person dresses, manner, signs of deference. “you can be a woman with a penis”, “woman” refers to gender and not the biological sex This vocabulary gap is responsible for nearly all disputes between groups on issues concerning this area.
