HIGH

The definition of high evolved from a description of elevation to a state of elation

I’m so proud of us as a society (yes cause my opinions are the validation people are yearning for), for using the phrase “I’m high on” for intangibles in life. High on success, high on love, high on a person. It takes the power away from hazardous substances and rightfully directs people to look for more wholesome paths of finding joy and peace in the midst of all that is evil in this world.

Unlike being happy which is a state or an emotion you feel at that moment, I like to believe a high is more of a place, a place where you’re untouchable, no one and nothing can bring you down, whatever brought you joy wasn’t a brief fleeting pleasure, it’s the kind of happiness that sustains you and protects from a world that at every other moment is pitted against you.

Being high on life is probably the highest level of all. Its when the simplest, most powerful and yet most underrated part of your existence, the very fact that you live to see the sun today, is the reason for your high. when you’re high on life truly high on life, however short that is, you experience a divinity that the poets described and Elvis sings about. This high opens your misery clouded eyes and lets you see the magic that exists in this world in contrast to the disinhibition that accompanies substance highs.

As rollercoasters have taught us everything that goes up comes back down. In times that aren’t filled with rainbows and unicorns, it’s important to carry the strength from the last happy place and hope for the next happy place. (And remember *insert motivational quote here*). It’s important to live life one high to the next and to accept the lows with grace.

Find the people and things that makes you feel like you’re out of this world,that make you feel invincible and do whatever it takes to keep them in your life cause that feeling, that high is the closest you’ll come to heaven on earth.
