THE NIGHTS ARE DISAPPEARING INTO THE LIGHT

Undeniably, the light bulb is by far one of the greatest inventions in history. With just a flick of a switch, we push away darkness. But the use of light bulbs comes with a price. The excess light we dump into the environment is endangering ecosystems by harming animals whose life cycles depend on the dark. We are also endangering ourselves by altering the biochemical rhythms that normally flow with natural light levels. The night sky is disappearing into the light.

OEDIPUS COMPLEX

The Oedipal complex, also known as the Oedipus complex, is a phrase coined by Sigmund Freud to describe a child's feelings of desire for his or her opposite-sex parent and jealously and hatred at his or her same-sex parent in his psychosexual stages of development hypothesis. In essence, a boy believes he is fighting with his father for his mother's affections, whereas a girl believes she is fighting with her mother for her father's affections. Children see their same-sex parent as a competitor for the attention and affections of their opposite-sex parent, according to Freud.
